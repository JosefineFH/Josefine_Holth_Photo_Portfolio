About me the photographer

I discovered my passion for photography when I was around 16 years old and got my first camera. I remember wanting my own camera for so long. I finally convinced my parents when we are on a holiday in Denmark. I don't remember what brand it was, but I remember the feeling of taking those beautiful photos.

When I was 20 something I got my first ever single mirror camera witch was a canon from the 2000 series with an 18 - 55mm IS II lens. I took it with me everywhere I went.

When I just got my second camera, the canon 600D, I started to get more serious about it. I took my first photo shoot ever in 2018, which was a maternity shoot of my sister, it became an even bigger interest for me.
In the last 3 years I have started to learn more and more about taking pictures. Now I have taken photos in weddings, maternity, family, children, events different for companies.

I love taking pictures that people can look back on and remember how good it was, how it felt and just be happy about what I created for them to remember those special moments.
