Let's talk about the numbers

Elopements / Wedding

1000,- kr | This is a 30 minute that fits for the one that doesn't want that many options.

2000,- kr | For the ones that want more than just the basic. This will be for 1 hour before or after the wedding.

Whatever you go for, we will find a location that is perfect for you, if you have a special place or many, you might have the perfect place in mind, or we can find something together.

And of course, this comes with a consultation hour in advance where we go over what you want and see for you.
I can also tailor packages if you want something more without this.


All these packages can also be combined, and of course, we can also extend them with photos during weddings or a half-day photoshoot from the whole day.

Family portraits

1500,- kr | We set aside one hour to take photos of the whole family, photos of the kids and some of just the parents. You can also take with you some changes of clothes.

Don't worry if you are afraid or worried that your kids will not collaborate with taking photos. If they want to play, walk on the beach, blow bubbles or bring balloons, we will do that.

It's all up to you. This is your time.

Maternity Photoshoot

1000,- kr | This is for 1 - 2 hours at a chosen location. It will be passable with several outfits if you want to. There are images of the expectant mom and the expectant dad together and separately if you want to. If this is not your first pregnancy, there will also be images with your kids as a family.

Portraits

This can be for engagement, anniversary or those of you who want photos with your loved ones.

1000,- kr | 30 minutes. This might be for the ones that don't need that many options.

2000,- kr | 1 hour. This is for the ones that want some more options. This can also be a newborn photoshoot if you want that. This happens when the child is 5 -14 weeks old.

As a photographer, I am most concerned with what you want from the photoshoot. There are many thoughts about investing in photos, such as that you might feel out of place or that the children will not cooperate. However, there is nothing to worry about. We agree on where you want to take pictures, whether it is in a fantastic location or your own home.

What I take pictures of are you as a family or you as a human being. So if it ends with laughter and fun, a walk on the beach, climbing trees, this is what I am looking for and what I want to capture in these pictures, laughter and love.
Just remember that no matter what you go for, this is an investment for the memories you want and remember and want to save so you can look back on.
